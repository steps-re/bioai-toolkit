# External Validation Data for Exopeptidase Degradation

Three papers with proteolytic stability data for validating degradation predictions.

## Files Downloaded

### Bottger et al. 2017 (PLOS ONE) -- FULLY OPEN ACCESS
- `bottger2017_main.pdf` -- Full article PDF
- `bottger2017_S1_Fig.tif` -- Schematic peptide-protease binding complex
- `bottger2017_S2_Fig.tif` -- RP-HPLC chromatograms of Onc18 metabolites across matrices
- `bottger2017_S3_Fig.tif` -- Api88, Api137, LVPR-Onc112 stability vs human alpha-thrombin
- `bottger2017_S1_Text.docx` -- Methods for alpha-thrombin stability assay

### Kohler et al. 2024 (ACS Pharmacol Transl Sci) -- CC-BY 4.0
- `kohler2024_main.pdf` -- Full article PDF (from PMC OA package)
- **SI NOT DOWNLOADED** -- `pt4c00503_si_001.pdf` (637 KB) blocked by Cloudflare on PMC/ACS
  - Contains: peptide synthesis/characterization, nano-LC/MS/MS, cleavage product comparison
  - Manual download URL: https://pubs.acs.org/doi/suppl/10.1021/acsptsci.4c00503/suppl_file/pt4c00503_si_001.pdf
